# President Trump Speaks to Putin for First Time Since Start of Iran War

By

Milàn Czerny

President Trump and Russian President Vladimir Putin spoke for about an hour on Monday, discussing the wars in Iran and in Ukraine, according to a Kremlin readout of the call.

The Russian president said he raised several proposals for achieving a quick settlement to the Iran war, and that he had been in contact with Gulf leaders as well as Iranian President Masoud Pezeshkian. The two presidents also spoke about Venezuela and energy markets, the Kremlin said.
