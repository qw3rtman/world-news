Caesars Casino Chain Gets Buyout Interest From Fertitta, FT Says - Bloomberg

By Rob Golum

February 26, 2026 at 9:07 PM UTC

Caesars Entertainment Inc., the large US casino operator, is considering takeover offers including a bid from billionaire Tilman Fertitta, the Financial Times reported, citing people with knowledge of the matter.

The company, which operates the namesake Caesars Palace and other properties, is exploring a sale as well as a possible management buyout, the newspaper reported.
