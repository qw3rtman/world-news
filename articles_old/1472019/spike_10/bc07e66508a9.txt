From Caution to Calculation: The Saudi Crown Prince's High-stakes Bet on Iran - Middle East News

Jack Khoury

March 02, 2026

In recent weeks, it appeared that quiet regional pressure was aimed at preventing U.S. President Donald Trump from striking Iran – out of concern over a broader Middle East conflagration and potential damage to oil and gas infrastructure. The most prominent public advocate of a diplomatic solution was Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, who declared that his country would not allow its territory to be used for a military move.
