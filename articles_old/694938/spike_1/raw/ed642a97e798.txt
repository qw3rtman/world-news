LEO Pharma A/S’s leadership has been engaging prospective investors in recent months as it advances preparations for a potential stock market listing this year, Chief Executive Officer Christophe Bourdon said.

The Danish drugmaker, which focuses on skin conditions, is positioning itself to “be ready when shareholders decide to push the button,” Bourdon said in an interview. He described feedback from investor meetings held at recent JP Morgan and Jefferies health-care conferences as encouraging, citing interest in the century-old company’s track record and market positioning.
