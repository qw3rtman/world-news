In his current term as president of the United States, Donald Trump is in the public eye almost daily: delivering speeches, holding press conferences, giving interviews. Judging by his body language, as well as by the way he talks about the crisis with Iran, Trump doesn't appear to be eager to attack. He may resort to military action, perhaps in the near future. The Israeli defense establishment is definitely ready for this, and is in an extremely high state of readiness. Meanwhile the president looks as though every bone in his body is shouting: Don't fall into that trap.
