Silver prices have turned volatile after a huge rally, as the U.S.-Iran conflict, rising oil prices, and shifting Federal Reserve expectations scramble precious-metals markets. (GEORG HOCHMUTH / APA / AFP via Getty Images)

Precious metals prices have turned volatile over the past month—and again this week—as investors struggle to parse the influence of safe-haven flows tied to the U.S.-Iran conflict and the likely impact of a surge in oil prices on inflation pressures, the dollar, and broader interest rate markets.
