# Gemini 3 Deep Think Shocked Everyone: Why Benchmarks Are the New Turing Test (ARC-AGI-2, HLE, MMMU-Pro, Codeforces Explained)

The AI landscape had become somewhat routine for a period, with models showing modest improvements in intelligence, speed, and tool usage. In the first half of February 2026, the frontier “felt” stable. Anthropic shipped Claude Opus 4.6 and OpenAI shipped GPT-5.3-Codex. Then, GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark arrived as a real-time coding variant (processing more than 1,000 tokens per second) in a research preview powered by Cerebras.

Then the ground moved. A “major upgrade” to Gemini 3 Deep Think was announced with benchmark results that read less like an incremental release and more like a discontinuity in the curve.
