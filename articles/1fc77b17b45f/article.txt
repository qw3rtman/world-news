CNBC Sport: Paramount WBD merger could change sports rights strategy

Key Points

- If Paramount shifts away from larger MLB or NHL packages, other companies such as Versant may have opportunities to gain some live sports inventory.
- Paramount President Jeff Shell's future role at the company is up in the air if a deal with WBD is approved.
- Paramount may not want the same type of sports rights that Warner Bros. Discovery's TNT Sports has acquired.
