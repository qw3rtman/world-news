Thoma Bravo has agreed a deal to acquire third-party logistics provider WWEX Group Inc. and merge it with an existing portfolio company.

The private equity firm plans to combine WWEX with Auctane Inc., an e-commerce shipping software maker, according to a statement Tuesday that confirmed a Bloomberg News report.
