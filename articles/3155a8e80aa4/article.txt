Stripe May Be Looking to Buy PayPal. What It Means for the Stock.

By Nate Wolf

Buying PayPal would be a big deal, given its $40.6 billion market capitalization. (Eric Piermont/AFP via Getty Images)

PayPal Holdings was one of the few stocks to have a good day on Monday, surging after a report that the payments company was attracting takeover interest. Wall Street is split on whether interested parties would be buying low, or taking over a flailing asset.
