# U.S. and Israel kill Iran’s Supreme Leader in massive multi‑city strike

A joint U.S.–Israeli strike hit more than a dozen Iranian cities, killing Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei and destroying key air‑defense, missile, drone, and nuclear‑related sites. Retaliatory fire has already been reported across the region, with threats toward U.S. troops and civilians.
