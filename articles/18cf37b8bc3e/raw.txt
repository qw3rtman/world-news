# OpenAI raises $110 bil. in funding from SoftBank, Amazon, Nvidia

US tech firm OpenAI says it has raised 110 billion dollars in a push for more investment. The money is coming from Amazon, SoftBank Group and semiconductor giant Nvidia.

OpenAI, which operates ChatGPT, made the announcement on Friday. Amazon is investing 50 billion dollars, while Nvidia and SoftBank are putting in 30 billion dollars each.

SoftBank says the move will increase its stake in OpenAI to about 13 percent, adding that the US firm is valued at 730 billion dollars.

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman said, "Building AI that works for everyone will require deep collaboration across the stack, and we're excited to do this together."

The race to develop generative AI is intensifying. OpenAI, Google and Anthropic are among the industry leaders.
