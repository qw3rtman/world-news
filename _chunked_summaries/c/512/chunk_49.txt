NSA’s portfolio has more than 1,000 properties, 69 million rentable square feet, 550,000 units, across 37 states and Puerto Rico; PSA will wholly-own 488 of the over 1,000 properties and focus on Sun Belt and core markets. Before closing, PSA and NSA OP limited partners will form a joint venture of 313 properties, 19.6 million rentable square feet across 28 states and Puerto Rico, valued at about $3.3 billion, with NSA OP unitholders owning about 80% and PSA 20%; the JV will be capitalized with $2.2 billion of secured debt, including about $240 million mezzanine from PSA, and operate at about 70% leverage. PSA said it has committed financing of $4.0 billion from Goldman Sachs Bank USA and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, and expects the deal to close in Q3 2026, after unanimous board approval and NSA equity-holder approval.
