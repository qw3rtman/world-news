Political dynamics in Taipei favor Beijing too: Xi and Chinese officials disdain President Lai Ching-te as more separatist than Tsai Ing-wen, while the KMT and Taiwan People’s Party control 60 of the legislature’s 113 seats versus the DPP’s 51.
