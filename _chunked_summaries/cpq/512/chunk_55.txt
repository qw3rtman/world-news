### Reuters, 2026-03-02, “Putin will relay UAE president's complaints about Iranian strikes to Tehran, Kremlin says”: Russian President Vladimir Putin spoke by phone on Monday with UAE President Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan and, the Kremlin said, agreed to relay the UAE leader’s complaints about “Iranian strikes against UAE territory” to Tehran. The UAE president said his country was not being used “as a base to attack Iran,” so the strikes “could not therefore be justified.” Putin said he was ready “to convey these signals to Tehran” and to “provide all possible assistance in stabilising the overall situation in the region”; both leaders agreed an “immediate ceasefire” and a “return to diplomacy” were needed. Putin also said work on Iran’s nuclear programme had been derailed by “an unprovoked act of armed aggression” by the United States and Israel, and thanked Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan for efforts to help Russian nationals in the current situation.
