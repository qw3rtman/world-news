(NYSE American: KAPA), a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on innovative cancer therapeutics, said it had entered binding terms to acquire CL-273 from Celyn Therapeutics, Inc., a privately held biotechnology company with principal offices in Dover, Delaware, and backing from OrbiMed and Torrey Pines Investment. Boral Capital, LLC as sole financial advisor.
